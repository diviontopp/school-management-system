61 Years of Excellence
With a rich experience in moulding the young generation, the Salesians of Don Bosco have established a next-generation school in Egmore, adopting the CBSE syllabus. We blend tradition with modern innovation to build an unparalleled educational foundation.
Holistic Education
We provide quality education of international standard with modern technology and state-of-the-art facilities designed for experiential learning. Our curriculum stretches beyond the classroom, ensuring students grow emotionally, physically, and spiritually.
Inspiring Leadership
Our aim is to groom young children into responsible citizens and leaders with a difference, guided by the Salesian spirit of St. John Bosco. Through mentorship and peer collaboration, every child is nurtured into a confident trailblazer.
Vibrant Campus
From our modern laboratories to expansive sports grounds, every corner of DBX is dedicated to nurturing the next generation. The energy on campus is palpable, fueled by student passion and educator dedication.
