The Admissions Journey

A simple, transparent process designed so families can feel confident every step of the way.

1

Online Registration

Submit the registration form online via our official portal or in-person at the admissions office. A non-refundable registration fee applies. Applicable for LKG admissions 2026–27.

2

Family Interaction

Shortlisted applicants are invited for a warm, conversational interaction with the school's leadership to understand the child's interests, strengths, and readiness for the Salesian environment.

3

Document Submission

Submit all required documents including the child's birth certificate, Aadhaar card, previous academic records, and passport-sized photographs as specified by the admissions team.

4

Enrolment & Fee Payment

Upon confirmation of admission, complete the enrolment formalities and pay the fees as per the schedule provided. Welcome to the DBX Egmore family!

Documents Required

Please keep these ready to ensure a smooth admission process.

Birth Certificate

Original and photocopy of the child's birth certificate issued by the Municipal Corporation.

Aadhaar Card

Aadhaar card of both the child and parent/guardian for identity verification.

Proof of Residence

Current address proof such as utility bill, rental agreement, or bank statement.

Previous Records

Progress report or Transfer Certificate from the previously attended school (if applicable).

Passport Photos

Four recent passport-size photographs of the student on a white background.

Health Records

Immunisation records and health certificate from a certified medical practitioner.

Frequently Asked Questions

Have a question? We've got you covered.

Is DBX Egmore affiliated to CBSE or State Board?

Don Bosco School of Excellence, Egmore is affiliated to the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE), Delhi, and follows the CBSE curriculum from LKG to Grade 12.

What is the age criterion for LKG admission?

For LKG admission, the child should have completed 3.5 years of age as on June 1st of the year of admission. Children born between November 1 – October 31 of the previous year are eligible.

Are there mid-term lateral admissions available?

Lateral admissions are subject to seat availability in the specific grade. Please contact the admissions office directly. A Transfer Certificate from the previous school is mandatory.

Does the school have a transport facility?

Yes, the school provides bus service covering various parts of Chennai. Routes and fee details are shared upon enrolment. Contact the school office for the latest route map.

Is there a scholarship or fee concession available?

The school considers fee concessions on a case-by-case basis for economically weaker sections. Please speak directly with the Principal's office for merit or need-based scholarship inquiries.
